Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how KanMind ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data of users ("you") who register and use our Kanban board service. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information in compliance with applicable data protection laws, including those applicable from Germany and the European Union.

We host our service on Render (render.com), which processes and stores data on our behalf. Please note that by using our service, some responsibility for data security and processing is shared with this hosting provider.

This policy aims to minimize our liability and clearly delineate responsibilities.

Information We Collect

When you register and use KanMind, we collect the following personal data:

  • Name and username
  • Email address
  • Password (stored securely and hashed)
  • Usage data about your interactions with the Kanban board (e.g., tasks created, modified)
  • Device and log information such as IP address, browser type, and access times

How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Kanban board service
  • Manage your registration, authentication, and account security
  • Improve our services by analyzing usage patterns
  • Communicate important updates, changes, or support information
  • Comply with legal obligations

Legal Basis for Processing

Our legal basis for processing your personal data includes your consent when you register and use the service, the necessity to perform the contract (providing the service), and compliance with legal obligations.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Data may be shared with trusted service providers, including Render as our hosting provider, who assist in operating our service, always under strict confidentiality agreements and limited to necessary data processing.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction, including secure password hashing and encrypted communications. However, please recognize that no method of transmission or electronic storage is completely secure.

Your Rights
